INTRODUCTION

Aazmeer, Ahsan, and Zaid are first-year Computer Science students at FAST University, Karachi, pursuing their passion for technology and innovation. As part of their Introduction to Communication Technology project, they are collaborating on creating a professional website that will serve as a platform to showcase their resumes. This project not only reflects their dedication to learning web development but also highlights their ability to work as a team to achieve a shared goal. Through this website, they aim to present their skills, academic achievements, and aspirations in a visually appealing and organized manner. This initiative is a stepping stone toward their journey of becoming proficient developers and establishing a strong foundation in the field of computer science.

OUR PROJECTS

BRICK BREAKER GAME

A classic arcade game where the player controls a paddle to break colorful bricks. Built with C, this project demonstrates our skills in game development, including collision detection and user input handling.

PORTFOLIO WEBSITE

A responsive and interactive website that showcases our individual skills, projects, and achievements. Built using HTML & CSS, it offers a clean layout and easy navigation. This project highlights our web development abilities, from creating a professional design to implementing dynamic features.
